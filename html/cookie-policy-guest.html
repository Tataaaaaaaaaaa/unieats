This Cookie Policy explains how UniEats ("we," "us," and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience when you visit our website and use our services. By using UniEats, you consent to the use of cookies under this policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device and remember your preferences, settings, and login information.

Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • To provide personalized services and improve your browsing experience
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • To ensure the security of our website and prevent fraud

How can you control cookies?

You have the right to control and manage cookies on your device. Most web browsers allow you to set preferences for cookies, including accepting or rejecting certain types of cookies. However, please note that blocking or deleting cookies may affect your experience on UniEats.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies: These cookies help us analyze website traffic and improve our services.
  • Advertising cookies: These cookies are used to deliver targeted advertisements based on your interests.
  • Preference cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and settings.

Updating our Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please check this page regularly for updates.
